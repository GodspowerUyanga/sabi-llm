# Lagos Foods Ltd — Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

## Working hours
Standard working hours are 08:30 to 17:00, Monday to Friday. Staff are entitled
to a one-hour lunch break. Remote work is permitted up to two days per week with
line-manager approval.

## Leave policy
- Annual leave: 21 working days per year, accruing monthly.
- Sick leave: up to 10 paid days per year; a medical note is required after two
  consecutive days.
- Maternity leave: 16 weeks fully paid. Paternity leave: 2 weeks fully paid.
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 5 working days in advance through
  the operations manager.

## Expense reimbursement
Employees may claim approved business expenses. Receipts must be submitted within
30 days. Claims above ₦50,000 require director approval. Reimbursements are paid
with the next payroll run. Personal travel and entertainment are not reimbursable.

## Refund and returns policy (customer-facing)
Customers may return unopened products within 14 days of purchase for a full
refund. Perishable goods are non-refundable once delivered unless they arrive
damaged, in which case a replacement or full refund is issued within 48 hours of
a reported complaint. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within
5 business days.

## Code of conduct
All staff must protect customer data, avoid conflicts of interest, and report
any suspected fraud to the compliance officer immediately.
