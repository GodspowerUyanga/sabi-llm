# Lagos Foods Ltd — 2026 Growth Strategy (Internal)

## Targets
The board has set a Q2 2026 revenue target of ₦52,000,000, representing roughly
15% growth over Q1. Gross margin should be held at or above 38%.

## Priorities
1. Expand the cold-chain delivery network to Ibadan and Abeokuta.
2. Launch the SME wholesale tier with 30-day credit terms for vetted buyers.
3. Reduce customer acquisition cost from ₦1,800 to ₦1,400 by shifting spend to
   referral programmes.

## Risks
- Diesel price volatility affecting logistics cost.
- Currency fluctuation on imported packaging.
- Concentration risk: the top three customers account for 28% of revenue.

## Key metrics watched monthly
Revenue, gross margin, on-time delivery rate (target 95%), refund rate (target
below 2%), and repeat-purchase rate (target above 40%).
